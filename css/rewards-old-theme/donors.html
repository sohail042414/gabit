The Top Donor Reward Program (TDRP)

This program was created to reward donors monthly for their acts of benevolence. However, only one donor receives the reward monthly, and this month, it just might be you.

A reward card is issued every 3o days with the value of the next reward. Below is the reward card for 30th of March, 2023. The monetary value on this card can be yours!
