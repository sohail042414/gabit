Terms & Conditions

Our Policies

  1. You will not be able to see your cumulative points adding up, but you and everyone else will see a view of your name and position on the list when you make top donor.

  2. You would be contacted by phone to be notified of your reward, and you would be required to consent to the recording of the call and the use of the recording for promotions.

  3. You would be required to consent to the use of your photo for promotional materials. This would involve signing of a photo-consent agreement.

  4. You would be required to present yourself for a photo-shoot as scheduled by the program before receiving your reward.

  5. After all conditions are met, you would allow for between 2 to 5 working days for your funds to be credited into your account or accept a cheque for the value if you prefer a cheque, and if it happens to be a cash reward.

  6. You would be required to post on your social media accounts and your Facebook timeline that you were rewarded for supporting fundraisers on Giveyourbit.

Rules of the Reward

  1. Every action must be completed before the point is allocated for the action. For example, if a visitor taps on the ‘Donate Now’ button without completing the donation, the points will not be allocated. Also, if a visitor taps on ‘Become a Support’ button without registering as a supporter, the points will not be allocated to the visitor.

  2. All actions have unique number of points; some a high number and some low, others have a very high number. The number of points for each action however, will not be revealed. But as a bonus, inviting a friend will offer a high number of points.

  3. To invite a friend, a visitor will first become a supporter, log into their dashboard, tap on the invite a friend link, and invite friends by email or SMS. The points for inviting a friend is low, but a very high point will only be allocated when the invited friend makes a donation.
