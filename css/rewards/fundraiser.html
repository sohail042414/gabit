TDRP for Fundraisers

The Top Donor Reward Program (TDRP)

This program was created to increase the chances of fundraisers reaching their goal and doing so in good time. The reward program however is optional; fundraisers can decide not to participate, but participation would greatly offer some leverage to the fundraiser and potentially help it reach its goal.

Usually, processing fee from all donations is 8% plus N5.00, but participating in the reward program would require additional fee to be removed from the donations made to your fundraiser; making the total deduction to be 11% plus N5.00. This however does not affect your goal amount, as the only effect is that deductions are made alongside your donations, but the platform still allows your fundraiser to reach its goal amount a hundred percent.

The advantage is that your fundraiser will most likely reach its goal, and on time. Fundraisers on the reward program reach their goal 80% more times than fundraisers that are not on the reward program. If your fundraiser is not already on the reward program, your goal might not be reached. And if your goal could be possibly reached, it would require a lot of extra efforts on your part to promote and campaign to get supporters, which may take way longer than you could plan for. But the reward program would potentially eliminate or greatly reduce the efforts on your side and propel you to your goal more swiftly.

With only two taps, your fundraiser will be included in the reward program and you would be thankful for it. Once your fundraiser is included in the reward program, the reward emblem will become visible on your fundraiser display.
